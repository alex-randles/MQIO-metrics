Introduction back to ToC

The metrics within this ontology are specifically designed to assess the quality of R2RML mappings. Furthermore, these metrics are defined as subclassess of the Data Quality Vocabulary (DQV) [DQV] metric class (dqv:Metric) which are grouped into quality dimensions (dqv:Dimension) and quality categories (dqv:Category).
